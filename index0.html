About us

Our Philosophy

UN MUNDO NUEVO was born from the need to create an environment where children can develop Spanish speaking skills though individually, personalized teaching at their own pace. UMN is contribute to the learning process of our children to prepare them for a more competition and multicultural environment, all of this will be accomplished by given them tools and necessary values for a successful path. The integral development at UMN exceeds the standards given by the department of human services by incorporating extra-curricular activities such as YOGA, DANCE, PHYSICAL EDUCATION, DRAMA, ARTS, TECHNOLOGY, CULTURAL AWARENESS, FIELD TRIPS, EVENTS and more...

Mission

UMN mission is to help children expand and activate their minds to organically cultivate celebration of diversity by immersing them in Spanish language.

Vision

Is to have a social impact on children’s education thought a high standard of quality. We strive for each child to acquire a multicultural mentality as well as teach them to adopt healthy habits and a sense of security thought our nurturing environment.

Safety First

Our center is purpose-built to foster an environment where the children feel comfortable, safe, and ready to learn. With attention to every detail we design our center to ensure and encourage child's natural curiosity and creativity. UMN building follows the highest security codes for the City of Apple Valley. We installed a new sprinkler system as well as a new Fire Alarm system to ensure the safety of our children and Staff..

Certified Teachers

Our teachers believe in our mission of early education in Spanish, we are proud that our employees truly loved children and have careers that reflect their passion. All UMN teachers have met the teaching requirements set by Minnesota for Early Childhood Educators. Our staff have: * Passed background checks which includes a FBI fingerprint check as well, through the Minnesota Department of Human Services. * Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Education. * Being certified in First Aid, CPR, AHT and SUID.

Quality

UMN have the highest standards for cleanliness, you can be sure that your child will be in a clean and fresh environment.

Infants

Program Description

Infants are rapidly adjusting to the new world around them, constantly taking in information to form a foundation for future growth and development. Our caring teachers provide a loving, dependable and trusting help each baby reaches their greatest potential in all areas including:

  • Language, Literacy and Communication
  • Cognitive Development
  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Motor Development, Health and Well-being

Age Group

We provide warm and nurturing care and education to infants ages 6 weeks through 16 months.

Toddlers

Program Description

UMN multicultural toddler curriculum are designed specially to provide a safe and enriched learning environment for each child help them reach their maximum potential in all areas of development:

  • Language, Literacy and Communication
  • Cognitive Development
  • Motor Development, Health and Well-being
  • Social and Emotional Development
  • Arts

Age Group

Children from 16 months through 3 years old.

Pre School

Program Description

UMN Preschool Program is designed for children who do not understand or speaks Spanish, but it is also perfect for native Spanish speaker. Our qualified teachers, strong curriculum and fascinating classroom environment all help foster a complete child development experience. UMN Preschool uses a curriculum that is standards -based. Children will have the opportunity to progress through all materials at their own pace. The curriculum has heavy emphasis on Math, Reading, Writing and Science Physical Education is also a very important component of each day. UMN focuses on the four areas of integral education:

  • Language Art Area
  • Cognitive Area
  • Social-Emotional Area
  • Physical Development Area (Gross and Fine Motor Skills)

Age Group

Children from 33 months through Pre-Kindergarten.

School Age

Program Description

UMN School Age Program promote the social, physical, educational and emotional growth of all children in a safe, caring, structured environment. The primary goal is to ensure all families have access to affordable, high quality before and after school programs promoting strong academy success, health and fitness, recreational activities and enrichment opportunities that nurtures all aspects of the development of children and youth.

Age Group

Children from Pre-Kindergarten.

Fun Summer Camp

Program Description

This unique Spanish Fun Camp is unlike any other summer camp. Summer is the perfect opportunity to combine recreational and educational activities for the school-age children. The kids who attend our Summer Camp will also participate in our annual anniversary show. Our Summer Camp includes:

  • Healthy Snacks
  • Field trips to learn about our community in an exciting and educational ways
  • Intercultural classroom experiences
  • Yoga class
  • Latin dance
  • Supplementary material, books, latin games, activities in Spanish and English

UMN Programs the children will develop an understanding of not only our multicultural society but also a better view of our rapidly changing world. This will not only have a positive impact in their future live but will allow them to become successful and productive members of society at an early age.

Age Group

Kids from 6 years old through 10 years old.
