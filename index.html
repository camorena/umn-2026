Our Philosophy

Un Mundo Nuevo provides a nurturing, family-centered environment where children from birth to five years old learn through play, exploration, and meaningful relationships. Full Spanish Immersion supports early language development while celebrating multicultural identities, emotional growth, and strong family connections. Children develop confidence, curiosity, and a lifelong love for learning in a warm and inclusive community.

Our Teachers

UMN is proud to have a team of dedicated and passionate teachers, all with Bachelor's Degrees in Education, inspiring every student to succeed.

Our Values

At UMN, we teach more than knowledge — we inspire honesty, integrity, respect, responsibility, safety, teamwork, friendship, and a love of self-improvement.

Spanish Immersion

Our full Spanish immersion program helps children develop bilingual skills naturally, opening doors to cultural understanding and cognitive benefits.
